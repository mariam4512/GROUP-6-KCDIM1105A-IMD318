Understanding Dry Skin

Characteristics

  • Feels tight and uncomfortable
  • Appears flaky or rough
  • Shows fine lines easily
  • Prone to irritation and redness

What Dry Skin Looks Like

Real example of dry skin condition

Common Signs to Look For:

  • Flaking and Roughness:

    Notice the visible flaking and uneven texture

  • Fine Lines:

    Dehydrated skin shows more pronounced lines

  • Color Changes:

    Areas of redness or uneven skin tone

If your skin shows similar signs, follow our recommended care routine below.
